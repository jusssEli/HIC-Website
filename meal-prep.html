Meal Prep Tips

Plan Before Shopping

Create a detailed shopping list based on your meal plan to avoid impulse purchases and ensure you have all necessary ingredients.

Invest in Quality Containers

Use glass or BPA-free containers that are microwave-safe, leak-proof, and portion-controlled for easy storage and reheating.

Freeze Extra Portions

Prepare extra servings and freeze them for future weeks when you might be too busy to cook from scratch.

Batch Cook Basics

Cook large batches of staples like rice, quinoa, or roasted vegetables that can be used in multiple meals throughout the week.
