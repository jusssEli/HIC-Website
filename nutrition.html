Nutrition Basics

Understand your body's needs and how to meet them for optimal health.

Why Nutrition Matters

Proper nutrition fuels your body, supports immunity, boosts energy, and lowers the risk of chronic diseases. Learn about the essential nutrients and how they contribute to a balanced diet.

Essential Nutrients

Protein

Daily Intake: ~50g

Builds muscles, repairs tissues, and supports enzymes and hormones.

Sources: Meat, beans, tofu, dairy, eggs

Carbohydrates

Daily Intake: 225-325g

Your body's main energy source. Choose whole grains for better nutrition.

Sources: Bread, rice, pasta, fruits, veggies

Fats

Daily Intake: 70g (healthy fats)

Supports cell growth, protects organs, helps absorb nutrients.

Sources: Avocados, nuts, olive oil, fish

Fiber

Daily Intake: 25-30g

Aids digestion and helps control blood sugar and cholesterol.

Sources: Fruits, vegetables, legumes, whole grains

Vitamins

Support immune function, energy production, and bone health.

Examples: Vitamin A, B12, C, D, E, K

Minerals

Regulate fluids, support bones, aid muscle function.

Examples: Iron, Calcium, Magnesium, Potassium

Water

Daily Intake: 2-3 liters

Regulates temperature, cushions joints, supports digestion.
